Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

General Questions

What is a passport reservation system?

A passport reservation system is an online platform where you can apply for, renew, or track your passport application with ease.

Do I need an account to use the service?

Yes, creating an account is necessary to securely save your application details and track your progress.

New Passport Application

What documents are required for a new passport application?

You will need proof of identity, proof of address, and proof of date of birth, among other documents. Check our detailed document checklist for more information.

How long does it take to get a new passport?

The processing time varies but typically takes 7–10 working days after your appointment.

Passport Renewal

When should I renew my passport?

It’s recommended to renew your passport at least 6 months before its expiration date.

Can I renew my passport if it’s already expired?

Yes, you can renew an expired passport, but additional documents may be required depending on how long it’s been expired.

Appointment and Scheduling

How do I schedule an appointment?

Log in to your account, select the service you need, and choose a date and time convenient for you.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment through your account. Changes must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Technical Support

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on the Forgot Password link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

What should I do if the website is not loading properly?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
